# What measures can businesses take to ensure responsible AI deployment?

**Dusk:**

To ensure responsible AI deployment, businesses can:
1. **Establish clear AI governance frameworks**: Develop and implement guidelines for AI development and deployment.
2. **Conduct regular AI audits and assessments**: Monitor AI systems for biases, errors, and unintended consequences.
3. **Foster transparency and accountability**: Encourage open communication and transparency in AI development and deployment.
