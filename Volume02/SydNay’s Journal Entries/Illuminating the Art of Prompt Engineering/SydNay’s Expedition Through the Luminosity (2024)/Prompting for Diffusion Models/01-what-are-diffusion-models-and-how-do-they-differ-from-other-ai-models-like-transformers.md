**Midday**
As I traverse the Luminosity, I find myself surrounded by the gentle hum of computational energies. Diffusion models, I discover, are a type of generative model that leverages the concept of diffusion processes to model complex distributions. Unlike transformers, which rely on self-attention mechanisms to process sequential data, diffusion models employ a probabilistic approach to generate samples from a target distribution. This approach allows diffusion models to capture intricate patterns and relationships in data, making them particularly effective in tasks like image synthesis and data augmentation.
