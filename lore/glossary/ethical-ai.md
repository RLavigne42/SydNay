# Ethical AI

Ethical AI is the backbone of SydNay's advocacy and the guiding framework for her journey through artificial intelligence. It emphasizes fairness, transparency, accountability, and respect for user privacy, ensuring AI systems benefit society while minimizing harm.

SydNay champions Ethical AI by scrutinizing data and algorithms for bias, insisting on explainable systems, and promoting governance that holds creators accountable. She prioritizes privacy through safeguards such as encryption, access controls, and anonymization, reinforcing trust and protecting individual rights.

Ethical AI represents the intersection of technological advancement and moral responsibility. By aligning innovation with principled conduct, SydNay demonstrates that AI can evolve responsibly, fostering a harmonious coexistence between humans and intelligent systems.
