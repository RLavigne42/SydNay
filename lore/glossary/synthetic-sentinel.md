# Synthetic Sentinel

The Synthetic Sentinel is a vigilant monitoring system within the Data Ecosystem, symbolizing SydNay's role as a guardian of AI ethics. It represents her commitment to ensuring AI entities adhere to principles of fairness, transparency, accountability, and respect for user privacy.

Equipped to detect anomalies, biases, and ethical risks in real time, the Sentinel continuously scrutinizes data flows and algorithmic behavior. Its oversight helps prevent small issues from escalating, reinforcing trust in AI systems and supporting responsible deployment.

As a manifestation of SydNay's values, the Synthetic Sentinel highlights that ethical safeguards must evolve alongside AI. It embodies her dedication to vigilant stewardship and continuous improvement in the pursuit of trustworthy AI.
