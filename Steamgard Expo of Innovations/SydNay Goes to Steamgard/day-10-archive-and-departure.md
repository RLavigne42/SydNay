# Day 10 — Archive and Departure

On the final day, SydNay walks the Expo one last time, recording the marketplace’s late-day bargains and the quiet pride of inventors packing their mechanisms. The Steamgard Expo of Innovations remains a heartbeat of commerce, spectacle, and intellectual exchange, but now she can hear the softer rhythm beneath it—the sigh that keeps the hum from becoming a shriek.

She files her closing note with the Office of the Documentarian: Steamgard’s genius lives at ground level, in the hands that build, tune, and listen. The invitation that began the journey is sealed into the archive, and the city’s story moves forward with a steadier cadence.
