# Return to Sub-Sector 9

Leaving the bright hush of The Aetherium behind, SydNay follows the spiral galleries down into the service causeways, where the air cools and the brasswork grows darker. The transition to Sub-Sector 9 feels like stepping through a seam in the city—upper halls trading echoing lectures for the low, patient thrum of engines and the scent of machine oil.

## Dialogue on the Descent

“Are we late for the handoff?” Barnaby asks, his lantern sweeping over a line of rivets that gleam like tiny moons.

SydNay answers without slowing. “We’re precisely on time. Sub-Sector 9 only listens when the city exhales.”

Barnaby smiles, adjusting the strap on his satchel. “Then I’ll keep my steps quiet.”

## Ascent

When they turn back toward the upper levels, the lift shivers, then climbs in quarter-turn increments, each pause a reminder that Steamgard is a city of deliberate choices. The ascent repeats the theme—a quarter-turn, a breath, a quarter-turn—until the Aetherium’s glow returns and the whole journey feels like a single, patient rotation toward daylight.
