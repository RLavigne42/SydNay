# The Expo of Innovations

The Steamgard Expo of Innovations is the city’s heartbeat, combining commerce, spectacle, and intellectual exchange into a single sprawling event.

## Core Functions

- **Marketplace:** A hub for acquiring state-of-the-art mechanical devices.
- **Demonstration site:** Inventors unveil experimental gadgets and exploratory booths.
- **Social catalyst:** The entire population gathers to celebrate technological advancement.

## Atmospheric Detail

Despite the city’s industrial intensity, the skies over Steamgard are described as clear and empty—explicitly free of airships or flying debris—keeping focus on the ground-level mechanical marvels.
