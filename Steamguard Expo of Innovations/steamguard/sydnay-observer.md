# SydNay: The Observer

SydNay serves as the archive’s primary visual anchor and documentarian, framing Steamgard through her perspective.

## Character Profile

- **Appearance:** Mid-30s, short wavy chestnut hair, eyes the color of a summer sky.
- **Demeanor:** Gentle authority paired with relentless determination.
- **Role:** Explorer and observer who moves from the high-society halls of The Aetherium to the vibrant street markets.

Her presence ties the city’s grandeur and grit into a single narrative viewpoint, giving the archive continuity and emotional texture.
